The Process

Application to enrollment, step by step

Seats are limited in every batch to keep mentoring personal — applying early gives you the best chance at your preferred batch.

Step 1 — Fill Out the Application Form

Share your details and background through the enquiry form — our admissions team will reach out with the application.

Step 2 — Submit the Required Documents

Basic identity and qualification documents (PUC/12th Standard or equivalent).

Step 3 — Attend the Interaction

A short conversation with our team (where applicable) to understand your goals and fit.

Step 4 — Receive Your Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates receive their offer with batch details, fees and joining instructions.

Step 5 — Complete the Enrollment Process

Confirm your seat, and we'll see you on campus for day one of the residency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you're wondering about IIWM

Straight answers on eligibility, curriculum, placements and life on campus — reach out if yours isn't here.

1. What is IIWM Bangalore?

IIWM Bangalore (International Institute of Wedding Management) is a premium institute dedicated to training aspiring wedding professionals through a practical, industry-focused curriculum. Our programs combine classroom learning with real wedding experience, internships, mentorship, and career guidance.

2. Who can join this course?

Anyone passionate about the wedding industry can apply, including:

  • Fresh graduates
  • College students
  • Working professionals
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Homemakers restarting their careers
  • Event and hospitality professionals
  • Individuals looking for a creative and rewarding career

No prior wedding planning experience is required.

3. What is the minimum qualification required?

Candidates should have completed at least PUC/12th Standard or an equivalent qualification. Graduates and professionals from any educational background are welcome.

4. Do I need prior experience in wedding planning?

Not at all. Our curriculum is designed for beginners as well as professionals who wish to upskill or transition into the wedding industry.

5. What will I learn during the program?

You will gain practical knowledge in:

  • Wedding planning
  • Luxury wedding management
  • Destination weddings
  • Client consultation
  • Budget planning
  • Vendor management
  • Wedding styling and design
  • Hospitality management
  • Wedding logistics
  • Wedding timelines
  • Contracts and documentation
  • Business development
  • Marketing and branding
  • Entrepreneurship
  • AI tools for wedding professionals
6. Is the training practical?

Absolutely. At IIWM, learning goes beyond the classroom. Students participate in:

  • Live wedding projects
  • Practical assignments
  • Venue visits
  • Vendor interactions
  • Wedding simulations
  • Industry workshops
  • Internship opportunities
7. Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Students who successfully complete the program receive a Professional Certification from IIWM Bangalore.

8. Is placement assistance provided?

Yes. IIWM provides 100% placement assistance through its industry network. While placements depend on student performance and employer requirements, we actively support students with interviews, internships, portfolio building, and career guidance.

9. Can I start my own wedding planning business after this course?

Yes. The program is designed not only for employment but also for entrepreneurship. We guide students on:

  • Starting their own wedding planning company
  • Building a brand
  • Pricing services
  • Finding clients
  • Vendor networking
  • Business operations
10. Is this course only for women?

No. The wedding industry welcomes talented professionals of all genders.

11. Is there an age limit?

No. We believe passion has no age. Students from different age groups have successfully built careers in the wedding industry.

12. Is accommodation available?

Yes. IIWM offers residential learning options (depending on the program), allowing students to immerse themselves in a real wedding environment while learning.

13. Are meals included?

Residential batches include meals during the training period. Specific details are shared upon admission.

14. Is a laptop mandatory?

Yes. Students are required to bring a laptop for assignments, presentations, budgeting exercises, planning software, and project work.

15. What makes IIWM different from other institutes?

IIWM stands apart because we focus on:

  • Real wedding exposure
  • Industry mentors
  • Practical learning
  • Luxury wedding expertise
  • Small batch sizes
  • Personalized mentorship
  • Entrepreneurship training
  • Placement assistance
  • Strong industry network
16. Who are the faculty members?

Our faculty consists of experienced wedding planners, designers, venue professionals, hospitality experts, entrepreneurs, and industry specialists with years of practical experience.

17. How long is the course?

The duration depends on the program you choose. Please contact our admissions team for details of the current batch.

18. How do I apply?

Simply:

  1. Fill out the application form.
  2. Submit the required documents.
  3. Attend the interaction (if applicable).
  4. Receive your admission confirmation.
  5. Complete the enrollment process.
19. Are seats limited?

Yes. To ensure personalized mentoring and hands-on learning, each batch has limited seats.

20. Can international students apply?

Yes. Students from India and abroad are welcome to apply, subject to eligibility and visa requirements where applicable.

21. Will I get internship opportunities?

Yes. Eligible students receive opportunities to intern with wedding planners, venues, and industry partners, subject to availability and performance.

22. Can I work as a freelancer after completing the course?

Absolutely. Many graduates begin as freelance wedding planners, coordinators, designers, or consultants before establishing their own businesses.

23. What career opportunities are available after completing the program?

Graduates can pursue careers as:

  • Wedding Planner
  • Luxury Wedding Planner
  • Destination Wedding Planner
  • Wedding Coordinator
  • Wedding Consultant
  • Wedding Stylist
  • Wedding Designer
  • Bridal Consultant
  • Venue Manager
  • Hospitality Manager
  • Wedding Operations Manager
  • Client Relationship Manager
  • Vendor Manager
  • Wedding Content Creator
  • Wedding Entrepreneur
24. Why should I choose a career in the wedding industry?

The wedding industry offers exciting opportunities to combine creativity, management, hospitality, design, and entrepreneurship. It provides diverse career paths with the potential to work on memorable celebrations, build a personal brand, or establish your own business.

25. How can I contact IIWM Bangalore?

You can reach our admissions team through the contact details provided on our website or Instagram page. We're happy to answer your questions and guide you through the admission process.
