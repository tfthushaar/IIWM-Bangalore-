Program Overview

Four pillars, one residential experience

The IIWM program is built around four commitments: you live where you train, you work on real weddings from day one, you're mentored by working industry professionals, and you graduate into a structured placement pathway rather than a certificate and a search.

Residential Campus

Live where you learn, fully immersed in the craft for the duration of the program.

Real Execution

Train on live weddings, not case studies — from concept through to send-off.

Industry Mentorship

Learn directly from working wedding planners, decorators and stylists.

Placement Pathway

Graduate into a structured placement pathway with premier wedding houses.

What You'll Learn

Skills built for the wedding floor, not the classroom

Every module is taught alongside a live execution component, so what you learn in the morning gets practiced on-site the same week.

  • Event design and concept development
  • Vendor sourcing and management
  • Floral styling and decor execution
  • Budgeting and client management
  • Day-of coordination under pressure
  • Building your own wedding business
