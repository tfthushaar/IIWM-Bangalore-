The Wedding Business School Professional Program

The Wedding Business School Professional Program is an intensive 30-day residential learning experience designed to transform aspiring professionals into industry-ready wedding professionals. Built around real-world execution rather than traditional classroom learning, the program combines expert-led instruction, practical application, industry mentorship, and live wedding exposure — so you don't just learn the theory, you experience the profession.

Curious what this looks like in practice? Read more on our blog for career guides, industry trends and insights from inside the program.

Personalised Mentorship

One on One Mentorship by Industry Experts

Learning at The Wedding Business School extends far beyond the classroom. Students are assigned mentors, ensuring personalised guidance throughout the program — with the option to choose a preferred mentor from our panel of industry experts, subject to availability. Your mentor supports you through projects, offers career guidance, shares real-world insights, and helps you build the confidence to enter the industry.

Learn Through Real Weddings

The complete lifecycle of a real wedding — not an observation

One of the defining features of the Wedding Business School experience is exposure to the complete lifecycle of a real wedding. Students don't simply observe — they participate. From the initial client consultation and concept development to planning, vendor coordination, logistics, production, guest management, execution, and post-event wrap-up, students witness how every element comes together to create a successful wedding. Each participant gains exposure to at least one complete wedding journey — invaluable hands-on learning that cannot be replicated in a classroom.

A Curriculum Designed Around Practice

Learn → Practise → Execute → Reflect

Every concept introduced in class is immediately reinforced through practical application, ensuring students develop not only knowledge but also confidence, leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills. By the end of the program, you won't just understand wedding planning — you'll have experienced what it takes to deliver one successfully.
