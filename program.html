The IIWM Professional Program

Learn. Experience. Execute. Excel.

The IIWM (International Institute of Wedding Management) Professional Program is an intensive 30-day residential learning experience designed to transform aspiring professionals into industry-ready wedding planners. Built around real-world execution rather than traditional classroom learning, the program combines expert-led instruction, practical application, industry mentorship, and live wedding exposure — so you don't just learn the theory, you experience the profession.

Personalised Mentorship

One mentor for every five students

Learning at IIWM extends far beyond the classroom. Every five students are assigned one dedicated industry wedding professional as their mentor, ensuring personalised guidance throughout the program — with the option to choose a preferred mentor from our panel of industry experts, subject to availability. Your mentor supports you through projects, offers career guidance, shares real-world insights, and helps you build the confidence to enter the industry.

Program Overview

Four pillars, one residential experience

The IIWM program is built around four commitments: you live where you train, you work on real weddings from day one, you're mentored by working industry professionals, and you graduate into a structured placement pathway rather than a certificate and a search.

Learn Through Real Weddings

The complete lifecycle of a real wedding — not an observation

One of the defining features of the IIWM experience is exposure to the complete lifecycle of a real wedding. Students don't simply observe — they participate. From the initial client consultation and concept development to planning, vendor coordination, logistics, production, guest management, execution, and post-event wrap-up, students witness how every element comes together to create a successful wedding. Each participant gains exposure to at least one complete wedding journey — invaluable hands-on learning that cannot be replicated in a classroom.

A Curriculum Designed Around Practice

Learn → Practise → Execute → Reflect

Every concept introduced in class is immediately reinforced through practical application, ensuring students develop not only knowledge but also confidence, leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills. By the end of the program, you won't just understand wedding planning — you'll have experienced what it takes to deliver one successfully.
