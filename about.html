Meet Lakshmi Keerthi

Founder, IIWM Bangalore

A Computer Science Engineer and IIM Bangalore alumna, Lakshmi Keerthi is an award-winning luxury wedding planner with over two decades of experience in creating exceptional celebrations across South India.

She built La'kiru into a trusted luxury wedding planning brand, trusted by families across the country for milestone celebrations, and went on to co-found Bougainvil'la, a luxury wedding venue in Bangalore known for its immersive, detail-led execution.

Having mentored dozens of young planners informally over the years, she founded IIWM Bangalore to formalize that mentorship — bridging the gap between classroom theory and the reality of live wedding execution, in a residential format where students learn by doing.

Our Mission

To give every wedding-industry aspirant the residential, hands-on training that classroom-only courses can't offer — pairing real execution experience with mentorship from working professionals, so graduates step into their first job already capable, not just certified.

Our Campus

IIWM Bangalore runs as a fully residential program: students live on campus for the duration of the course, training on real weddings at luxury venues around Bangalore rather than simulated case studies — the same immersive model that shaped the founder's own career.
