Course Curriculum

A 30-day, module-by-module build

Each week pairs classroom fundamentals with live execution on an active wedding, so every concept is tested on-site within days of being taught.

Week 1 — Foundations

Event design principles, the wedding industry landscape, budgeting basics, and your first vendor site visits.

Week 2 — Styling & Decor

Floral styling, décor concepts, mandap and stage design, hands-on styling sessions on a live wedding.

Week 3 — Coordination & Vendors

Vendor management, run-of-show planning, day-of coordination under a mentor's supervision at a real event.

Week 4 — Business & Placement Prep

Client management, pricing your services, building a portfolio from your live-execution work, and placement interviews.

Internship

Shadow, then lead

Every student is paired with a mentor planner for the duration of the program. You start by shadowing on live weddings, then take on progressively larger responsibilities — by week three, most students are running elements of an event under supervision.

Placement

A pathway, not a certificate

Internship performance feeds directly into IIWM's placement pathway with partner wedding houses, decor studios and venues — placement assistance continues after graduation until you're placed.
