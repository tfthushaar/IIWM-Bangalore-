Meet Lakshmi Keerthi

Founder, IIWM Bangalore

A Computer Science Engineer and IIM Bangalore alumna, Lakshmi Keerthi is an award-winning luxury wedding planner with over two decades of experience in creating exceptional celebrations.

After building La'kiru into a trusted luxury wedding planning brand and co-founding Bougainvil'la, a luxury wedding venue in Bangalore, she founded IIWM Bangalore to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real wedding execution.
