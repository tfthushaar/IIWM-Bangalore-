Founder, The Wedding Business School

Meet Lakshmi Keerthi

Luxury Wedding Planner & Educator

Watch Video

Lakshmi Keerthi is an Award-Winning Luxury Wedding Planner with nearly two decades of experience in luxury and destination weddings.

A Computer Science Engineer and IIM Bangalore alumna, she chose to follow her passion for creating unforgettable celebrations and has successfully planned and executed weddings across India.

After building La'kiru into a trusted luxury wedding planning brand and co-founding Bougainvil'La, a luxury wedding venue in Bangalore, Lakshmi gained an insider's perspective on every aspect of the wedding industry.

Having worked closely with clients, venues, vendors, and wedding teams for years, she recognized a significant gap in wedding planning education — aspiring planners were learning theory but missing real-world exposure. This vision led to the establishment of The Wedding Business School, Bangalore, where individuals don't just learn about weddings and events but get to experience the process.

Beyond Theory. Into Experience. Toward Excellence.
