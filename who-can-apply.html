Minimum Qualification

PUC / 12th Standard or equivalent. Graduates and professionals from any background are welcome.

Minimum Age: 18

Applicants must be 18 or older — beyond that, there's no upper limit. Students of every age group have built wedding careers with us.

All Genders Welcome

The wedding industry welcomes talented professionals of all genders.

International Students

Students from India and abroad may apply, subject to eligibility and visa requirements.

Good to Know

A laptop is all you need to bring

Students bring a laptop for assignments, presentations, budgeting exercises, planning software and project work. Everything else — stay, meals, venue transport and training materials — is part of the residential program.

Limited Seats

Small batches, by design

To ensure personalized mentoring and hands-on learning on live weddings, every batch has limited seats. If you're ready, don't wait for the deadline.
